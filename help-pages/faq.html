How do my credits work?

1 Identified Visitor = 1 Credit. Credit lasts for 24 hours per Identified Visitor to capture unlimited real-time page visit data. If they come back more than 24 hours later, a new credit will be used.

How can I change my plan?

In order to select a different plan, click on “Products” in your dashboard. You will find your current plan titled “You are currently on this plan.” Next, select a different plan than your current plan and click on the dropdown to choose your number of credits and then click “Change to this plan.”

How can I change my number of credits?

Click on “Products” in your dashboard. You will find your current plan titled “You are currently on this plan.” Next, click on the dropdown to choose your number of credits and then click “Change to this plan.”

What happens if I run out of credits?

Don’t worry! You won’t stop receiving data. Instead, when you run out of credits your extra usage protection kicks in.

How does the Extra Usage work?

The extra usage balance is designed as a protection if you go over your credit limit. Don't miss out on leads when your website volume spikes. For every credit above your limit, the extra usage balance will increase by $0.XX depending on your selected plan. When the extra usage balance reaches $100, the balance will be charged and reset to $0. If $100 is not reached within the billing cycle, the balance will be charged at your next monthly billing cycle.
