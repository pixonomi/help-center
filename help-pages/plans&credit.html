Credits and Usage:

  • Each identified web visitor consumes one credit.
  • One credit grants 24 hours of unlimited real-time page visit records per identified visitor.
  • If a visitor is identified multiple times within a 24-hour period, only one credit will be deducted, regardless of how many times they visit.
  • However, if the same visitor returns and is identified again after 24 hours have passed since their initial visit, an additional credit will be deducted.

Choosing Credits:

  • It's recommended to start with half of your unique visitors per month, as we typically identify around 50% of online visitors per website.
  • For example, if your average monthly unique visitors are 10,000, selecting 5,000 credits would be suitable.
  • Plans are easily adjustable in your dashboard, and your trial period can help determine the best fit.

Extra Usage Protection:

  • In the event of credit depletion, our extra usage protection activates to ensure uninterrupted data reception.
  • The extra usage balance incurs a nominal charge for additional credits utilized, safeguarding against exceeding your credit limit.
